The artist behind the landscapes

Barbara Van Dromme

Barbara Van Dromme is a contemporary arboreal artist inspired by forests, trees, water and the changing qualities of natural light.

Her work explores the quiet relationship between landscape and memory. Through painting, she seeks moments of stillness: reflections on water, the presence of trees and the atmosphere of woodland spaces.

Each artwork is an invitation to slow down and reconnect with the natural world.

Artist Statement

"Painting is a way of listening to the landscape. Trees, water and light become a language through which emotions and memories can appear."
